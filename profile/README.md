# ComplyTime Labs

> The experimental arm of [ComplyTime](https://github.com/complytime) — where new ideas are prototyped, tested, and validated.

**complytime-labs** is the incubation space for the ComplyTime project. This is where we explore new tooling, integrations, and compliance workflows before they graduate to the main [complytime](https://github.com/complytime) organization.

## What's Here

* **[gemara2oscal](https://github.com/complytime-labs/gemara2oscal)**: A transpiler for converting the Gemara logical model to OSCAL.
* **[creme-brulee](https://github.com/complytime-labs/creme-brulee)**: ComplyTime Skills Discovery.
* **[baseline-demo](https://github.com/complytime-labs/baseline-demo)**: OSPS baseline demo.
* **[vagrant-boxes](https://github.com/complytime-labs/vagrant-boxes)**: Automation for creating vagrant boxes.
* **[cac-content](https://github.com/complytime-labs/cac-content)**: CaC content fork.
* **[oscal-content](https://github.com/complytime-labs/oscal-content)**: An OSCAL content repository with test data for ComplyTime.

## Relationship to ComplyTime

Projects here are experimental and may change rapidly. Once validated, they may be promoted to the main [complytime](https://github.com/complytime) organization or stay here for training and development. For stable, production-ready components, see the [ComplyTime org](https://github.com/complytime).
